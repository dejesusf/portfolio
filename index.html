FELICLARE DE JESUS

My name is Feliclare De Jesus and I am a Student Full Stack Web Developer enrolled in the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp. Also, you may call me Feli (it's like Kelly, but with an F).

I was born and raised in Hawai'i, on the busy island of O'ahu. No, I don't know how to surf and that's because I'm afraid of deep water. But, I do love going to the beach and just soaking in the sun and salty air. Nowadays, I mostly spend days off lounging with my dogs, Miso and Yuri, streaming a show on Netflix and crocheting. I am a huge fan of Pokemon and have been since 1998!

I recently moved to Washington in March 2020--right before quarantine. Washington is not new territory as I previously attended Washington State University as an undergraduate. That is where I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Biology. I once had hopes of becoming a doctor, but towards the end of receiving my Bachelor's degree my plans changed. In 2018, I started my career in laboratory medicine.

As of April 2020, I've been employed with the University of Washington's Department of Laboratory Medicine & Pathology. My work as a Clinical Laboratory Technician has been fulfilling but I am looking for change. My strengths lie in frontend web design, however I am really excited to learn more of the backend portion of web development!

Please checkout my portfolio and ongoing projects to see what I'm currently working on. You can also follow me on Github.
